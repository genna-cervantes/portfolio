# Why Developer Experience Matters

Developer experience is easy to treat as polish, but it directly affects how quickly a team can understand, change, and trust a system.

## Feedback Loops

Small improvements compound:

- Clear linting rules reduce decision fatigue.
- Fast checks make feedback loops easier to keep.
- Predictable workflows help engineers focus on product behavior instead of process.

## Invisible Work

The best developer experience work often feels invisible once it is done. That is usually the point.

## What I Notice

I tend to look for repeated friction: setup steps that are hard to remember, slow checks that discourage validation, and unclear errors that make debugging feel heavier than it needs to be.
